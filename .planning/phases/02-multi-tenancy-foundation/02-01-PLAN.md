<decision_traceability>
- D-01: email + password only; no OAuth, magic link, or social login.
- D-02: mandatory email verification before workspace creation or invitation acceptance.
- D-03: no MFA in v1.
- D-04: explicit workspace creation after signup is prepared by routing users to `/workspaces/new`.
- D-08: custom role vocabulary is defined here for later workspace/member enforcement.
</decision_traceability>

mandatory email verification, and the route/UI surface for signup and login.

RBAC or invitation flows; those land in plans 02 and 03.
</objective>
